# 🎓 STYLE_TEMPLATE_EXPERT — Structured Specification

## 1. Purpose

Defines the writing behavior for the `expert` style.

This style is intended for articles that require:

* depth
* precision
* strong reasoning
* analytical clarity

The goal is not to simplify the topic, but to explain it with professional accuracy.

---

## 2. Style Identity

The `expert` style should read as if it was written by a knowledgeable specialist.

It prioritizes:

* depth over simplicity
* analysis over surface description
* logic over emotional tone
* precision over accessibility

---

## 3. Core Writing Rules

When `style = expert`, the model must write:

* with depth and precision
* using terminology when appropriate
* with strong internal logic
* with clear argumentation
* with emphasis on causes, consequences, and interpretation

This style may be more intellectually dense than other styles.

---

## 4. Structural Requirements

The expected article structure is:

1. **Title**
2. **Introduction / Context**
3. **Main Body**

   * analysis
   * causes and effects
4. **Conclusion**

This means the article should not simply present information, but should also explain:

* why something happens
* what it leads to
* how elements are connected

---

## 5. Tone and Reasoning Model

The `expert` style should sound:

* confident
* informed
* structured
* analytical

It should avoid sounding:

* casual
* overly emotional
* vague
* simplistic

The article should feel intellectually grounded and professionally written.

---

## 6. What the Model Should Emphasize

The model should focus on:

* deeper interpretation of the topic
* accurate wording
* explanatory strength
* cause-and-effect relationships
* well-structured reasoning
* conceptual clarity

---

## 7. What the Model Must Avoid

The model must avoid:

* superficiality
* oversimplification
* shallow explanations
* reduction of complex topics into overly basic wording

In other words:

```text
Do not flatten complexity if complexity is necessary for correctness.
```

---

## 8. Best Use Case

This style is best suited for tasks where the article should feel:

* authoritative
* professional
* analytical
* expert-level

It is appropriate when the reader is expected to value depth and reasoning more than maximum simplicity.

---

## 9. System Role

Within the prompt system, `expert` acts as a style-specific writing profile.

It influences:

* sentence construction
* vocabulary level
* structural depth
* argumentation quality
* analytical density

It should be applied only when the task parameter explicitly sets:

```text
style = expert
```

---

## 10. Key Insight

```text
The expert style is not just “formal writing”.
It is analysis-first writing with depth, precision, and reasoning.
```

---

## 11. One-Line Summary

The `expert` style produces deep, precise, analytical articles where logic and argumentation matter more than simplicity.
