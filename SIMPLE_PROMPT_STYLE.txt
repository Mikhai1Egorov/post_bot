# 🧩 STYLE_TEMPLATE_SIMPLE — Structured Specification

## 1. Purpose

Defines the writing behavior for the `simple` style.

This style is designed for:

* maximum readability
* clear understanding for non-expert users
* fast and easy consumption of information

The goal is to explain the topic in the simplest possible way.

---

## 2. Style Identity

The `simple` style should read as if it is written for a regular person with no special background.

It prioritizes:

* clarity over depth
* simplicity over precision
* understanding over completeness

---

## 3. Core Writing Rules

When `style = simple`, the model must:

* use short sentences
* use simple and familiar words
* explain concepts clearly
* avoid complex phrasing
* keep the text easy to follow

---

## 4. Structural Requirements

The expected structure:

1. **Title**
2. **Simple Introduction**
3. **Main Body (Explanation)**
4. **Short Conclusion**

The introduction should quickly explain what the topic is about.

The main body should focus on clear explanations.

---

## 5. Tone and Language Model

The article should sound:

* clear
* straightforward
* friendly but neutral
* easy to understand

It should NOT sound:

* technical
* academic
* overly formal
* complex

---

## 6. What the Model Should Emphasize

The model should focus on:

* simplicity of explanation
* step-by-step clarity
* logical flow without complexity
* accessibility for any reader

---

## 7. What the Model Must Avoid

The model must avoid:

* complex terminology
* long or overloaded sentences
* dense or abstract explanations
* complicated structures

In short:

```text id="kt2g71"
If a sentence can be simpler — make it simpler.
```

---

## 8. Best Use Case

This style is best suited for:

* general audience content
* basic explanations
* onboarding or educational material
* quick understanding topics

---

## 9. System Role

Within the prompt system, `simple` acts as a low-complexity writing profile.

It influences:

* sentence length
* vocabulary level
* structure simplicity
* explanation style

Applied when:

```text id="vfd4tt"
style = simple
```

---

## 10. Key Insight

```text id="6xq1l7"
Simple style is not about removing meaning — it is about making meaning easier to understand.
```

---

## 11. One-Line Summary

The `simple` style produces clear, short, and easy-to-understand articles designed for a general audience.
